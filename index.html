The Fe Maidens is a team of passionate young women dedicated to empowering the next generation of female engineers. Through robotics, our members learn essential skills for becoming effective leaders in STEM. Based at The Bronx High School of Science in Bronx, NY, we compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition each year.
