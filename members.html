Hello from Team 2265!


Captain
Violet Killymore_vert
Hi! I'm Violet Killy.close

I've loved technology since my early years of editing goofy home movies and taking apart the VCR with a screwdriver. When I joined the Fe Maidens as a sophomore, my hobby evolved into a passion for all things STEM. I started off as a programmer, and after rising through the ranks I was elected Captain. The past three years have been incredible! It's one thing to learn about this stuff in a classroom setting, but there's something special about putting your skills to the test and building something. On top of that, I've loved working with such an amazing group of girls who are passionate about the same things that I am. And nothing beats the moment when, after six frenzied weeks of non-stop build, fifty girls erupt in cheers as the robot finally comes to life. In addition to giving me technical and leadership experience, this team has boosted my confidence and inspired me to pursue a career in STEM. And I know that I'll always look back on the hours we've spent building robots and building a team as some of my best.

The Electronics department is responsible for the wiring and electrical components of the robot. The team members in the Electronics have to put their heads together to figure out the correct amounts of electronic connections needed to make the robot work. On top of this, members of electronics are responsible for building a test robot, in order to make sure everything is functioning properly before the construction of the actual robot. The Electronics members do a great job of wiring the robot every year.
